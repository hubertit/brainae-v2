# Suggested Features for Brainae University Website

## High Priority (Essential)

### 1. **Admissions Section** ⭐⭐⭐
- Application process and requirements
- Deadlines and important dates
- Required documents checklist
- Application form/integration
- **Why**: Critical for prospective students

### 2. **Contact/Inquiry Form** ⭐⭐⭐
- Contact form with validation
- Department-specific contact options
- Inquiry categories (Admissions, General, Financial Aid, etc.)
- **Why**: Better than just email/phone, captures leads

### 3. **Faculty Directory** ⭐⭐
- Faculty profiles with photos
- Department filtering
- Research interests
- Contact information
- **Why**: Builds credibility, helps students connect

### 4. **Financial Aid & Scholarships** ⭐⭐⭐
- Available scholarships
- Financial aid application process
- Tuition and fees information
- Payment options
- **Why**: Critical decision factor for students

## Medium Priority (Important)

### 5. **Campus Facilities/Virtual Tour** ⭐⭐
- Photo gallery of facilities
- Library, labs, sports facilities
- Virtual tour (360° or video)
- Campus map
- **Why**: Helps students visualize the experience

### 6. **Research & Publications** ⭐⭐
- Research centers and labs
- Recent publications
- Research projects
- Faculty research profiles
- **Why**: Important for graduate students and reputation

### 7. **Alumni Success Stories** ⭐
- Alumni testimonials
- Career outcomes
- Notable alumni
- Alumni network information
- **Why**: Social proof and inspiration

### 8. **Campus Life** ⭐
- Student clubs and organizations
- Events calendar
- Student services
- Housing information
- **Why**: Shows student experience beyond academics

## Recommended Implementation Order

1. **Contact Form** (Quick win, high value)
2. **Admissions Section** (Critical for conversions)
3. **Financial Aid Section** (Decision factor)
4. **Faculty Directory** (Builds trust)
5. **Campus Facilities** (Visual appeal)
6. **Research Section** (For graduate programs)
7. **Alumni Stories** (Social proof)
